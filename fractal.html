Fractal

This was a project I worked on with a Bio-engineering student during my last semester at Berkeley. It's based off a technique to detect bone fractures using a tuning fork and stethoscope (read about that here: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2681212/).

The code and enclosure files live here: https://github.com/IDD-su18/fractal.
